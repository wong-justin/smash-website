  Fan Contributions to Smash Series

Some smash fans make fan art in the form of videos. The community produces hundreds of videos on a range of smash subjects. Here is a popular combo that has been animated by Youtuber Applesgosh. Warning: this video is LOUD.

Members of the smash community are even inspired to make their own games. Project M (M for Melee) is a modified version of Super Smash Bros Brawl with increased pace and a larger roster.

Here are some resources created by the Smash Bros community:

Smash Wiki
Comprehensive listing of smash games and details
Vods.co
Videos of tournament matches
Smashladder
Play smash games online with other users
SmashBoards
The longest running Smash forum
Super Smash Flash 2
Fan game based off the original series but played in a web browser
Rivals of Aether
An indie platform fighting game in the same genre as Smash Bros, created by some with a background in smash
